Pixel Art
◤ about
A set of pixel art icons made for digital UI and UX use. The main design challenge with pixel art icons is legibility at small sizes — shapes need to read clearly at a glance without relying on fine detail. Each icon was iterated on until it felt immediately recognisable, while keeping a consistent visual weight and style across the whole set.
